Schoenfeld et al., 2017, J Sports Sci
题目：Dose-response relationship between weekly resistance training volume and increases in muscle mass（系统综述+元分析）
DOI: 10.1080/02640414.2016.1210197

文献内容
- 纳入 15 项研究、34 个训练组，做每肌群“周总组数”与增肌的剂量-效应元回归。
- 每增加 1 组（/周/肌群），效果量增加约 0.023（约 0.37% 的肌肉增长提升）。
- 在同一研究内，高组数比低组数的效果量差异约 0.241（≈3.9% 增长差），显示“多组”总体更优。
- 将周总组数分为 <5、5–9、≥10 组三档时，高组数趋势最佳（P≈0.074），提示 10+ 组/周/肌群通常优于低组。

文献结论
- 存在“递增型”剂量-效应：更多周总组数通常带来更大肥大，但样本主要覆盖至 10+ 组/周，超高量区间证据有限。
- 实践上，可将 10+ 组/周/肌群视为中高量参考，结合频率拆分与恢复监控，避免一次性堆量导致质量下滑。

English Summary
- Systematic review + meta-analysis (15 studies, 34 groups) on weekly sets per muscle vs hypertrophy.
- Each +1 set/week/muscle → ES +0.023 (~0.37% gain); higher vs lower volumes differ by ES ≈0.241 (~3.9%).
- Bins <5, 5–9, ≥10 sets: ≥10 shows best trend (P≈0.074); evidence up to mid-high volumes, ultra-high not well tested.

English Conclusions
- Graded dose-response: more weekly sets generally yield more growth up to ~10+ sets/week; evidence sparse beyond that.
- Treat 10+ sets/week/muscle as mid-high reference; split frequency and monitor recovery to avoid single-session quality drop.
